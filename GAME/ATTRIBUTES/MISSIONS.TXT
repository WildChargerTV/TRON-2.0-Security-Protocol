** M I S S I O N

**
**
**
** A WORD OF WARNING
**
** This file's documentation is woefully out of date with the code that
** referencens it.  Most of the documentation _should_ be accurate, but
** unfortunately this cannot be gauranteed.
**
**
**
**
**
** Mission definitions specify what levels are in the game.  The 
** ordering of the missions are the same as they appear in this file
** (Mission0, Mission1, Mission2, etc.)
**
** All mission definitions should contain the following properties:
**
**	NameId (Integer)
**
**		The Id (stored in CRes.dll)	of the text string containing the
**		name of the mission.
**
**	SystemNameId (Integer)
**
**		The Id (stored in CRes.dll)	of the text string containing the
**		name of the current system. (used on the subroutine menu)
**
**	BriefingId (Integer)
**
**		The Id (stored in CRes.dll)	of the text containing a detailed
**		explanaation the mission.
**
**	ObjectiveIds (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of Ids (stored in CRes.dll) of objectives
**		known at the start of the mission.
**
**	Photo (String)
**
**		The file name for photo associated with the mission
**		(e.g., interface\0000club.pcx).
**
**	DefaultWeapons (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of weapon names that should be chosen by default
**		for the mission.  Like subroutines, a build (alpha, beta,
**		gold) and/or corruption state (normal, infected/corrupted) may be added.
**		(e.g., "Disc,Sequencer gold,Cluster beta infected,BallLauncher" ).
**
**	IdealWeapons (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of weapon names that should the player would
**		ideally have at the beginning of a mission. 
**		(e.g., "Disc,BallLauncher" ).
**
**	IdealSubroutines (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of subroutine names that the player would ideally
**		have at the beginning of a mission.  Optionally, a build (alpha, beta,
**		gold) and/or corruption state (normal, infected/corrupted) may be added.
**		(e.g. "Sequencer,Cluster gold,Shield_prism infected, Flash_buffer beta infected" ).
**
**	IdealProcedurals (String)
**
**		A comma seperated list of procedural names that the player would ideally
**		have at the beginning of a mission.
**
**	IdealBuildPoints (String)
**
**		The number of build points the player will ideally have at the beginning
**		of this mission/level.
**
**	DefaultGadgets (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of gadget names that should be chosen by default
**		for the mission. 
**		(e.g., "Camera Disabler,Code Breaker" ).
**
**	DefaultAmmo (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of ammo names that should be chosen by default
**		for the mission. 
**		(e.g., "Camera Disabler,Code Breaker" ).
**
**	DefaultMods (String)
**
**		A comma separated list of ammo names that should be chosen by default
**		for the mission. 
**		(e.g., "Camera Disabler,Code Breaker" ).
**
**	Level0-??? (String)
**
**		The file name for each level (scene) file (e.g., Worlds\m01s01).
**		associated with this mission.  Multiple levels may be specified
**		(e.g., Level0 = "xxx", Level1 = "yyy", etc.).
**	
**		NOTE:  the .dat extension should NOT be included.
**
**	SubroutineConfig (Bool)
**
**		SubroutineConfig = 0 (or 1): Setting this to 1 on a mission will, as 
**		soon as the first map loads, take the player to the subroutine screen 
**		where the subroutines can be set up for a new system memory configuration
**	
**	Reward0-??? (String)
**
**		Specification for the experience rewards available in this mission.
**		Each specification consists of a name and a value separated by a comma.
**		(e.g., Reward0 = "xxx,50", Reward1 = "yyy,10", etc.).
**
**	Level(x)NameId (Integer)
**
**		The Id (stored in CRes.dll) of the text string containing the
**		name of the level.
**
**	SystemMemory(x) (String)
**
**		If specified with a number (ex: SystemMemory2), this will be the
**		system memory map for that level.  If no number
**
*/
